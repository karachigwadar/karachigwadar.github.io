Company Profile

Built on Trust, Driven by Excellence

Karachi Gwadar International Logistics (Pvt) Ltd. stands as a pillar of reliability in Pakistan's logistics landscape. As a fully licensed and bonded freight forwarding company, we operate with the highest standards of professionalism, integrity, and operational efficiency.

Our strategically positioned headquarters on MT Khan Road, Karachi — the heart of Pakistan's commercial hub — gives us direct access to Port Qasim and Karachi Port, enabling rapid turnaround times for both import and export consignments. With Gwadar Port emerging as a game-changer in regional trade, our company name reflects our forward-looking vision to connect Pakistan's southern corridor with the world.

Under the leadership of our Managing Director, Nabila Younus, we have built a team of seasoned professionals who bring decades of combined experience in customs regulations, international trade compliance, and multi-modal transport coordination. Every shipment we handle is backed by meticulous planning, transparent communication, and an unwavering commitment to delivering your cargo safely and on time.
